About TFP Renovations

A Team You Can Trust in Your Home

TFP Renovations Ltd — formerly known as The Female Plumber & Renovations Ltd — is run by Lauren Hudson, a fully qualified and experienced plumber and renovation specialist based in Ripley, Derbyshire.

Lauren and her skilled team bring a meticulous, personal approach to every project. From a single shower installation to a full luxury bathroom overhaul or whole-house renovation, the team treats every home with respect — working around you, solving problems as they arise, and never cutting corners.

As a female-led trade business, Lauren understands the importance of trust, clear communication, and feeling comfortable with the people working in your home. That’s why so many of her clients come through personal recommendation, and why the team holds a perfect 5-star rating across every Google review.
